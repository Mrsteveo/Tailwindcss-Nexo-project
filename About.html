PAGEANT REVOLUTION!

SUBSTANCE AND POWERFUL PLATFORM

After seven-year management of Miss Eastlands, we are celebrating 8 years in the pageant and modeling industry. We’ve conducted in-depth research into the operations of five major pageants, paying particular attention to the needs of modern contestants. We discovered that for over a century, pageants have been running the same way: one king/queen gets all the attention and the other contestants are sent home, sad, crying and confused, their chapters are closed when the event ends. While most of the world’s pageants are continuing along a traditional path, today’s young contestants have changed. They are intelligent and results-oriented, they demand substance, and they don’t buy artifice. Pageant give Models and Actors a powerful platform to brand themselves, get noticed and use the pageant powerful marketing platform to excel in their carrier.

LEADERSHIP COACHING, DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM AND EMPOWERMENT

Our pageant and modeling format that includes leadership coaching, development program and empowerment plan attracted the attention and support from powerful brands such as PAM NAIL LAQUE, BRANDME KENYA, JEFFERY WILSON SHOW, GREENSPAN MALL, DAVANA HOTEL and many more…

Our pageant got honoured with endorsements and praises for our humanitarian work, contribution to young contestants as well as our impact on lives of vulnerable youth and young at most risk lactating mothers around Eastlands from dignitaries, prominent members of local and international communities, government officials, and many more.

SWIMSUIT COMPETITION

The swimsuit competition is a prominent segment of the MISS EASTLANDS® pageant as most of our prospective delegates are committed to health and fitness. We provide exceptions for girls that cannot participate in the swimsuit competition with no judging scores deducted. In other words, you don’t get penalized for not participating. Swimsuits can be traditional, or they can reflect the active wear trend as long as they consist of a short top and a short bottom. You are also welcome to use one piece swimwear.
